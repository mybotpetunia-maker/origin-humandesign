The five types

Which one
are you?

Human Design divides all people into five types. Knowing yours changes how you approach decisions, work, and relationships.

Manifestor 9% of people

Built to initiate and create impact. Most powerful when acting independently — but needs to inform others to avoid resistance.

Generator 37% of people

Sustained life-force energy. Made to respond, not initiate. Happiest when doing work that genuinely lights them up.

Manifesting Generator 33% of people

Fast, multi-passionate, non-linear. The rules don't apply to them the way they apply to others — and that's the point.

Projector 20% of people

Here to guide and direct others. Deeply perceptive. Works in short bursts of focused energy — not designed for the 9-to-5 grind.

Reflector 1% of people

Deeply sensitive to environment. A mirror for community health. Decisions require a full lunar cycle — 28 days — to land correctly.
